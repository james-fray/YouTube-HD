Preferred Quality:

  1. YouTube HD always tries to set the video quality based on your settings. This might not be your preference if video quality is already acceptable.
  2. This is useful to allow reducing the quality later by the user.
  3. Do not force the quality when a higher quality is selected.
  4. Use this option for debugging purpose. If enabled, the extension prints the current video quality and whether the quality change is necessary or not.
